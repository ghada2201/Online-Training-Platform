Continuous Evaluation and Feedback

(Training Coordinators, HR, and IT Departments)

Welcome to the Continuous Evaluation and Feedback module. This module focuses on gathering ongoing feedback through surveys to help evaluate the effectiveness of our training programs and guide future improvements.

  • Gather continuous feedback from participants to assess the effectiveness of training.
  • Use feedback to identify areas for improvement in training content and delivery.
  • Ensure that the training program remains relevant and impactful through ongoing evaluation.

Key Focus

  • Feedback Surveys
  • Continuous Monitoring and Improvement
  • Participant Engagement and Input

This module is ongoing and centers around the use of feedback surveys to ensure that our training programs meet the needs of all participants. Your input is invaluable in helping us improve.
