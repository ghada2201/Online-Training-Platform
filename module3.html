Role-Specific Training

(Finance/HR/Customer Service)

Welcome to the Role-Specific Training module tailored for Finance, HR, and Customer Service departments. This training will address the unique social engineering threats relevant to each department and provide best practices for protecting sensitive information.

Training Objectives

  • Address the unique social engineering threats relevant to each department.
  • Teach employees how to handle and protect sensitive information within their department.
  • Foster collaboration between departments and IT/security teams to enhance security measures.

Key Topics

  • Customised Scenarios for Your Department
  • Best Practices for Protecting Sensitive Information
  • Collaboration with IT and Security Teams

This module will take approximately 1.5 hours to complete. Please ensure that you are prepared to fully engage with the content and participate in the interactive elements.
