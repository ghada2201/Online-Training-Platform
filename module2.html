Advanced Technical Training

(Technical Staff/IT Module)

Welcome to the Advanced Technical Training module designed specifically for IT and technical staff. This training will equip you with the knowledge and skills required to recognise, respond to, and mitigate advanced social engineering attacks.

Training Objectives

  • Understand advanced social engineering techniques, including spear phishing, vishing, and deepfakes.
  • Learn how to effectively respond to social engineering incidents and contain potential threats.
  • Explore technical safeguards and tools to prevent and detect social engineering attacks.
  • Participate in hands-on simulations to practice identifying and responding to advanced phishing attempts.

Key Topics

  • Deep Dive into Advanced Social Engineering Techniques
  • Incident Response and Containment Strategies
  • Technical Safeguards Against Social Engineering
  • Advanced Phishing Simulations

This module will take approximately 2 hours to complete. Please ensure that you are prepared to fully engage with the content and participate in the interactive elements.
